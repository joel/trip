The Hidden Valley

Summit Reflections

Standing at 3,000 meters, the perspective shifts. The worries of the valley floor seem insignificant. We sat in silence for an hour, watching the shadows stretch across the peaks. This is why we travel—to be reminded of how small we are, and how vast the beauty of the world is.
